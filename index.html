Welcome

Hello,

I am Kavinda Herath currently pursuing MSc. in Mechanical Engineering at Politecnico di Milano. I was born in Sri lanka and grew up in Kurunegala. I attended the Royal International School in Kurunegala from the Nursery until Ordinary Level exam. In school, I served as the president of student association for one year and served as a senior prefect in the school. Further I was a member in college swimming squad and participated in several extracurricular activities. After succesfully passing the G.C.E O/L exam I moved to the high school, St. Anne's college Kurunegala. In high school, I was a member in the english debating club. I did my G.C.E. Advance Level exam in Physical Stream and sucessfully passed it.

I moved to Italy, Turin order to follow my passion in mechanical engineering. I obtained Bachelor of scienece in Mechanical Engineering from Politecnico di Torino. During my bachelor, I did a exchange program to University of Arkansas in United States of America and completed it with a GPA of 4.0/4.0. I also served as a member of student team R.G. Glide polito team. I did an experimental thesis for my bachelor titled 'The mechanical properties of natural fiber and a case study of Coconut fibers'. In above thesis, I was testing the possibilty of using extracted coconut fibers to make biocomposite materials, resistant to cold and wet condition.

I enrolled into Politecnico di Milano to pursue my Master's. In PoliMi, I particpated in the programme of passion in action and earned a open bage in bioinspired robotics. I participated in the 20/21 erasmus program in Brno university of technology and succefully completed. Currently, I am working on my master thesis on design, implementation and control of bioinspired snake like acquatic robot. I am expecting to graduate in 2021. And my future interest is to work in the fields of Robotics, mechatronic and dynamics engineering.

Thank you.

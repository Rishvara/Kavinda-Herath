Hello,

I am Kavinda Herath currently doing MSc in Mechanical Engineering at Politecnico di Milano. I was born in Sri lanka and grew up in Kurunegala. I attended the Royal International School from the Nursery until Middle school. While study in school I served as the president of student association for one year and served as a prefect in the school. Further I was a member in college swimming squad.After passing the middle school exam I moved to the high school St. Annes school Kurunegala to attempt the Advance Level. While in the high school I was a member in the debating club. I did my G.C.E. Advance Level exam in Math Stream and sucessfully passed it.

I moved to Italy, Turin order to do my bachelor. I obtained my bachelor in Mechanical Engineering. While doing my bachelor I did a exchange program to USA for a one semester and served as a member in R.G. polito glide team.
